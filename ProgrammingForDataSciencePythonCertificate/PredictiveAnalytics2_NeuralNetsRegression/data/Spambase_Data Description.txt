
Variable Name			Description
make				% of words in the e-mail that match "make"
CAP_avg				average length of uninterrupted sequences of capital letters
CAP_long			length of longest uninterrupted sequence of capital letters
CAP_tot				total number of capital letters in the e-mail
Spam				1 = spam 0 = not spam
 

Source:  UCI Machine Learning Repository, HP database of emails
