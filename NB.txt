Naive Bayes Classifier
what is Naive Bayes Classifier======
based on Bayes theorem which gives the conditional probability of an event A given B.
P(A|B) = P(B|A)P(A)/P(B)
when is NB used======
face recognition, email spam identify, medical diag, news category, shopping buy or not.
Advantage======
easy, less training data, scalable, fast, not sensitive to irrelevant features
